There are 7 types of brand names

  1. Eponymous

    2. Give your own name, in case you have big enough ego

  2. Descriptive name

    3. Like American airline

  3. Acronomyc

    4. In case you want to hide true first impresion about your product

  4. Suggestive: real, composite or invented

    5. with real will be hard with copyrights, composite way easier, and with invented - easiest

  5. Associative

    6. Describe your brand with something like Amazon. The biggest river is also the biggest retailer

  6. Also non English words

    7. Samsung, Lego and so on..

  7. Abstract

    8. Rolex, Kodak.. takes power from phonetics
